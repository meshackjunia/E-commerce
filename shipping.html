Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

1. Shipping Options & Delivery Times

OJmesh Enterprise offers several shipping options to meet your needs:

Standard Shipping

Delivery Time: 3-5 business days

Cost: $5.99 (Free for orders over $50)

Our most economical option for non-urgent deliveries.

Express Shipping

Delivery Time: 2-3 business days

Cost: $12.99

Priority handling for faster delivery.

Overnight Shipping

Delivery Time: Next business day

Cost: $24.99

For urgent orders placed before 12 PM EST.

*Delivery times are estimates and begin when your order ships, not when you place your order. Some remote areas may experience longer delivery times.

2. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days after payment is received. During peak seasons (November-January), processing may take 3-5 business days.

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

3. International Shipping

We currently ship to the following countries:

  • United States
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Australia
  • Germany
  • France

International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Additional customs fees, taxes, or duties may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking number. You can track your package:

  1. Using the tracking link in your email
  2. Through your account on our website under "My Orders"
  3. Directly on the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, or USPS)

5. Shipping Restrictions

Some products have special shipping requirements:

  • Electronics: May require signature upon delivery
  • Large items: May ship via freight carrier with additional fees
  • Hazardous materials: Some items cannot be shipped internationally

Any shipping restrictions will be clearly noted on the product page.

6. Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable (due to incorrect address, refusal by recipient, etc.), we will contact you to arrange reshipment. Additional shipping fees may apply.

7. Shipping Address Changes

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact us immediately at shipping@ojmesh.com or call +123 456 7890. We cannot guarantee address changes once an order has entered the shipping process.

8. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us within 7 days of delivery (or expected delivery date for lost packages). We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and arrange for replacement or refund as appropriate.

9. Holiday Shipping Schedule

During major holidays, shipping carriers may have modified schedules that affect delivery times. We will post holiday shipping deadlines on our website and in checkout during these periods.

Questions About Shipping?

Our customer service team is happy to help with any shipping inquiries.
