Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and express shipping (1-2 business days). Shipping costs vary based on the size and weight of your order, as well as the destination.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most items. Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached. Some items like electronics may have different return policies.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for qualified businesses. Please contact our wholesale department through the contact form or email wholesale@ojmesh.com for more information.
