Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

1. Return Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Eligible Items:

  • Unused products in original condition
  • Items with original packaging and tags
  • Defective or damaged items (see section 3)

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Digital products (e-books, software downloads)
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Perishable goods
  • Intimate or sanitary products
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"

2. How to Return an Item

Follow these steps to return an item:

1

Initiate Your Return

Log in to your account and go to "My Orders" to start the return process. Alternatively, contact our customer service.

2

Package Your Item

Include all original packaging, accessories, and documentation. We recommend using the original packaging if possible.

3

Ship Your Return

Use the provided return label and ship your package. For items over $50, we recommend using a trackable shipping service.

4

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

3. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We may require photos of the damage and will provide instructions for return or replacement.

4. Return Shipping

Free Returns: We provide free return shipping for defective, damaged, or incorrect items.

Customer-Paid Returns: For returns due to change of mind, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless you selected our "Free Returns" option at checkout.

5. Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Please note that your bank or credit card company may take additional time to post the refund to your account.

6. Exchanges

To exchange an item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures you receive the quickest possible service.

7. International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any applicable customs fees. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

8. Holiday Return Policy

Items purchased between November 1 and December 31 may be returned until January 31 of the following year.

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer service team is happy to assist with any return questions.
