How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Proceed to checkout when ready
  3. Enter your shipping and payment information
  4. Review your order and confirm your purchase

You'll receive an order confirmation email with all the details.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it by contacting our customer service at support@ojmesh.com or calling +123 456 7890.

After this window, your order may already be in processing and cannot be modified.

What shipping options do you offer?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days ($5.99)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($12.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($24.99)

Free standard shipping is available for orders over $50.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to:

  1. Track your package directly on our website under "My Orders"
  2. Visit the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, or USPS) and enter your tracking number

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. To be eligible for a return:

  • Items must be unused and in their original condition
  • Original packaging must be included
  • A receipt or proof of purchase is required

Some items are non-returnable, including digital products, perishable goods, and personalized items.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return:

  1. Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Follow the prompts to generate a return label
  4. Package your return and attach the label
  5. Drop off at the designated carrier location

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • OJmesh Gift Cards

Is it safe to enter my credit card information on your site?

Yes, your security is our top priority. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your information. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.

All payments are processed through PCI-compliant payment processors.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot password?"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the instructions to create a new password

How do I update my account information?

You can update your account information at any time:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Update your personal information, shipping addresses, or payment methods
  4. Click "Save Changes"
